About Me

I’m a Minecraft-style 3D artist focused on **Blockbench**. I build baby mobs, furniture, weapons, and props with clean topology and texture-friendly UVs. My goal: models that feel **cute**, **optimized**, and **ready to drop** into your world.

Open for collaborations, commissions, or community projects. Ping me and let’s craft something awesome!
