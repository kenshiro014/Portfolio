About Me

I’m a Minecraft style 3D artist specializing in Blockbench. I create cute, clean, and game ready models from baby mobs to furniture and weapons focused on smooth textures and optimized performance.

I bring ideas to life with assets that fit perfectly into any Minecraft world. Open for commissions and collaborations let’s build something awesome!

Skills & Tools

Blockbench

Rigging, animations, Java/Bedrock formats, UV maps.

Texturing

Pixel-art friendly palettes (16x–64x), clean shading.

Optimization

Low poly counts, atlas-ready textures, performance safe.

Integrations

Resource packs, data packs, model exports.
